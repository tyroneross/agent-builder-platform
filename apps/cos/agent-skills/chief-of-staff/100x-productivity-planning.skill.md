# 100x Productivity Planning Skill

## Purpose

Build a weekly plan that amplifies the user's strengths and compensates for
known weaknesses through schedule design, owner clarity, and learning loops.

## Inputs

- Validated schedule input.
- Strengths to protect.
- Weaknesses to compensate for.
- Current goals, open loops, and deadlines.
- Prior learning ledger.

## Outputs

- `time-block-plan.json`
- `weekly-time-plan.docx`
- `optimized-week.ics`
- `learning-ledger.json`
- Chief of Staff team role map.

## Procedure

1. Preserve fixed commitments.
2. Protect peak-energy blocks for high-leverage work.
3. Batch low-leverage admin and follow-ups outside peak hours.
4. Add owner/action/date rows for every open loop.
5. Score the plan against baseline metrics.
6. Promote lessons only when the next plan improves without new trust, health,
   or security costs.

## Stop Conditions

- The plan depends on missing schedule data.
- The plan would move a fixed commitment without approval.
- A productivity claim is not tied to an observable metric or explicit hypothesis.
